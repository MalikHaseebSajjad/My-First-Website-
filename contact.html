Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for store credit, or you can opt for a refund minus shipping costs.

How long does shipping take?+

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for delivery within 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by location.

Do you offer international shipping?+

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You can view the full list of countries during checkout.

How can I track my order?+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Do you have physical stores?+

Yes, we have flagship stores in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago. Visit our store locator page for addresses and opening hours.
